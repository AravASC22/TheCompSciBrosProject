Coding Bootcamps

Enrolling in a coding bootcamp is another up and coming way to transition into the field of computer science and technolgogy. Coding bootcamps are accelerated programs where subjects such as Full-Stack Web Development, Data Science, Digital Marketing, and UX/UI Design are taught. Many people who attend these bootcamps may have had a technical background in other subjects such as math, physics, biology, chemistry, etc, or are transitioning from one field into the tech industry. Coding bootcamps usually have an intensive curriculum that can consist of website development programming languages like JavaScript, Ruby on Rails, PHP, and other languages such as Java and C#.
